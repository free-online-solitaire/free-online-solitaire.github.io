Snow Rider 3D

Snow Rider 3D is a thrilling online snowboarding game that lets you race downhill, dodge obstacles, and collect gifts as you go. Enjoy a fast-paced, winter wonderland adventure right in your browser—no download required.

How to Play Snow Rider 3D

  1. Press "Play" and use the arrow keys or WASD to steer your sled left and right.
  2. Hit the up arrow or W to jump over obstacles.
  3. Avoid trees, snowmen, and rolling snowballs to keep your run going.
  4. Grab gifts to unlock new sleds and rack up a higher score.
  5. Try to travel as far as you can without crashing!

Controls

  • Left Arrow / A – Move Left
  • Right Arrow / D – Move Right
  • Up Arrow / W – Jump
  • On Mobile – Swipe left/right, tap up to jump
