Do you love speed, excitement, and creativity? Then PolyTrack is the perfect game for you! It’s a car racing game where you can zoom through thrilling tracks filled with loops, jumps, and sharp turns. But there’s a twist, you can also design and build your own tracks! PolyTrack was created by Kodub developer and released in March 2023 . Available on both desktop and mobile browsers , it’s easy to access and super fun to play. Whether you enjoy racing against the clock or testing your creativity by designing challenging tracks, PolyTrack will keep you entertained for hours. PolyTrack is more than just a racing game, it’s also a platform for creativity. You can: Race on tracks designed by you, the game’s creators, or other players. Use the built-in track editor to design your dream racing circuit. Share your creations with others in the PolyTrack community. Compete to set the fastest times and climb the leaderboards. The game’s simple yet addictive mechanics and vibrant community make it a must-play for anyone who loves racing or designing.

  1. Exciting High-Speed Racing: Feel the rush as you race through fast, action-packed tracks with loops, jumps, and tricky turns. Every millisecond counts as you aim for the best time!
  2. Creative Track Building: PolyTrack lets you be both a racer and a creator. Use the easy-to-use track editor to design tracks with challenging obstacles, ramps, and sharp curves.
  3. Vibrant Community: Join the PolyTrack community to share your designs, try tracks built by other players, and compete for the top spot on the leaderboard.
  4. Cross-Platform Accessibility: No downloads or installations are needed! PolyTrack works smoothly on web browsers for both desktop and mobile devices, so you can play anytime, anywhere.
  5. Low-Poly Graphics: The game’s minimalist, low-poly art style ensures smooth performance while adding a unique visual charm.
  6. Practice with Premade Tracks: If you’re new to track building, start with premade tracks to get the hang of the game. As you improve, dive into creating your own masterpieces! Playing PolyTrack is super easy! Follow these steps to get started: Choose a Track : Pick from premade tracks or create your own using the editor. Build Your Track : Add loops, ramps, and obstacles to make your track exciting and challenging. Test Your Track : Use the Test Drive feature to see how your track plays. Start Racing : Race against the clock to set your best time and compete with others. Whether you’re racing on your own designs or tracks made by others, every race is a new challenge! For Racing WASD or Arrow Keys : Drive your car Enter or R : Restart the race. For Track Building Left-Click : Place track pieces. Mouse Scroll : Zoom in or out. Shift + Scroll : Move items up or down. R or Space : Rotate items. T : Test your track. Desktop Mobile Tablet
  7. How do I play PolyTrack? PolyTrack is simple! You can race on tracks or design your own. Try to set the fastest time and challenge yourself or other players.
  8. Do I need to build tracks to enjoy the game? Not at all! PolyTrack offers premade tracks , so you can jump straight into racing if you don’t feel like designing.
  9. Is PolyTrack free to play? Yes, it’s completely free! You can play it directly in your web browser without any downloads.
  10. Can I play PolyTrack on my phone? Absolutely! PolyTrack works on both desktop and mobile browsers, so you can enjoy it anywhere.
